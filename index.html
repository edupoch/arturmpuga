Artur M Puga is a Spanish producer, dj and sound designer. His work is focused on the intersection between academic electronic music, club oriented genres and traditional rhythmic structures from African and Asian heritage. Throughout his career he has released two albums with labels Eternal Recordings and Arkestra under his own name, appeared in several compilation albums, done soundtrack scoring for films & live performers and sound design for A/V installations, as well as being a regular DJ on the Spanish club scene.

He performed and exhibited his work in places such as Society of Arts and Technology Montreal, Korea National University of Arts, WOS Festival, HKCR (with his own show as resident DJ), Codex Club Berlin, IFSV Tokyo, La Casa Encendida Madrid, Café OTO, Radio Primavera Sound, NTS and Noods Radio to name a few.
